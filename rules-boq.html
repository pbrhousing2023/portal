PBR Bachelor/ Bachelorette Officers Quarters (BOQs) Rules and Regulations

website copy published on January 22, 2023
BOQ Bunkhouses Engineers Accommodation

The following rules and regulations shall serve as guidelines by the occupants to ensure their stay enjoyable, comfortable, secured and safe, and to maintain order and help preserve the facilities.

1. The BOQ is part of the PBR Housing Compound Complex. As such, BOQ residents are also bound under the Rules and Regulations on the use of other facilities in the Housing Compound such as Clubhouses, Auditorium, Guesthouses, Swimming Pools, Badminton Court, Tennis Court, Gym, Basketball Court and Golf Course.

2. Transferring of furniture and appliances from one location to another location is prohibited.

3. All bunkhouses including enclosed area at Arellano Hall are non-smoking area. Smoking is only allowed outside BOQ buildings.

4. Sounds of musical devices or TV should be toned down especially at night time so that occupants that wish to take early rest will not be disturbed. All unnecessary noise should be avoided by 10:00 PM.

5. Use of the telephone (personal calls) should be limited to ten (10) minutes per call to give way to emergency calls from the plant and to give other bunkhouse occupants opportunity to use the telephone.

6. Everyone should cooperate in keeping the place clean and orderly at all times. The caretakers will be responsible in maintaining the cleanliness of toilets/bathrooms, common kitchen and reception rooms. However, cleaning and maintaining order of occupied rooms will be the responsibility of the occupants. Caretaker is also responsible in the waxing of the floor of occupied rooms once a month.

7. Unnecessary noise or disturbance that may cause inconvenience to other guests must be avoided. Likewise, guests will be held liable for any damage to the guesthouse property due to their unacceptable behavior.

8. Bed sheets, blankets, pillows and pillowcases will be provided to every room. Proper care of linens must be observed. Changing of linens is done once a week. Clean linens will be provided by the caretaker, while the occupant is responsible in the replacement.

9. Toilet papers are to be provided. These should not be brought to other locations.

10. Medicine cabinets are provided in every bunkhouse. Replenishment of medicines shall be the responsibility of the bunkhouse coordinator. Medicines can be withdrawn from the Clinic. Proper use, consumption and disposal should be observed.

11. Presence of termites and other pests/destructive insects should be reported immediately to the General Services Supervisor for proper control and abatement.

12. Bunkhouse coordinator must prepare a work request for the repair of any appliances and facilities.

13. Pets are not allowed at the BOQ area.

14. To promote good relationship, cooperation and unity, occupants are expected to observe proper decorum at all times.

15. When leaving BOQ permanently, all cabinets, drawers and door keys should be turned over to the General Services Supervisor.
