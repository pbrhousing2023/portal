PBR Guesthouse House Rules and Regulations

website copy published on January 22, 2023
Guesthouses Guests Visitors Accommodation

To help us maintain the orderliness and cleanliness of the facilities and amenities, kindly follow our house rules.

1. Strictly no approved reservation, no venue and/ or accommodation usage. For confirmed reservations, check-in and check-out notification of all guests should be provided by the host/ proponent to PBR General Services.

2. For the security of your valuables, please bring them with you or put them inside your locked luggage. PBR will not be liable for the loss of any valuables.

3. In cases of reservation concerns, emergency, incidents or abnormalities, you may call and report to PBR General Services thru the telephone operator no. 047 244 3300 local 0 or 30337. And mobile numbers 0926 292 3593 for PBR Operator, 0969 495 4482 for General Services.

4. Breakage and missing items in the guesthouse will be charged to guests host/ proponent. Guests are expected to observe good housekeeping and comply with no plastic/ styro policy.

5. Guesthouses are provided with air conditioning system, fans and other appliances. Guests are welcome to use them. However, please turn-off all electrical appliances after using.

6. Guest should observe water conservation measures. Please make sure faucets are closed after used. And report water leakage in the area.

7. Unnecessary noise or disturbance that may cause inconvenience to other guests must be avoided. Likewise, guests will be held liable for any damage to the guesthouse property due to their unacceptable behavior.

8. Laundry services is not provided during your accommodation. If deemed really necessary, please coordinate with your host/ proponent to help you.

9. Strictly no cooking at the Guesthouse area. Meals should be brought in or pre-ordered from Guesthouse Concessionaire.

10. Gambling, any other illegal activities, firearms and explosive materials are prohibited inside the company premises. Similarly, pets are not allowed. Smoking as well as heavy drinking of liquor are not allowed inside the rooms of Guesthouses. Likewise, unauthorized stay of persons not declared in the guests list are also prohibited.
